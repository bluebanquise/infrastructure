Home

BlueBanquise is an opensource project, based on the wish to provide a simple but flexible stack to deploy and manage cluster of servers or workstations.

The stack is using Ansible, and relies heavily on inventory merge hash_behaviour and groups.

BlueBanquise was originaly made for the Algoric HPC center, before opening to all.

System administrator should find here all needed documentation, files, or contacts to take the most of the stack.
